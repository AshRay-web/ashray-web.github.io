02

Completed Plasma Physics MATLAB

Kinetic Trajectory Simulation of Dusty Plasma

Computed the modified Bohm sheath criterion for dusty plasma and developed MATLAB code incorporating Orbital Motion Limited (OML) theory. Studied variations in plasma density, particle behavior, and electric potential — comparing trajectories with and without dust particles to showcase the importance of dust effects in KTS modeling.

Supervisor: Dr. Roshan Chalise — St. Xavier's College, Kathmandu

Mathematical Modeling of Snow Mountain Dynamics

Developed a steady-state mathematical model for snow accumulation and melting on mountainous terrain. Built a Python-based numerical solver to simulate snow-packed mountain dynamics, parameterizing environmental factors including precipitation rate and solar radiation.
